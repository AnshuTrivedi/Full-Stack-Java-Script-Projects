Tahri the veg biryani

Detailed steps to parepare delicous Veg Biryani

Tahri is first choice when weather is cold or you have craving for spicy and healthy food.I am addicted to this healthy and easy to cook recipy, I'm sure once you make it you will crave for it again. Sharing ingredients list and cooking steps to prepare dish.

Ingredients

  • Green vegetables like green chilli, Round gaurd, Potato, tomato, Ridge Gaurd, musrooms and anything you like to add
  • Garlic, Onion
  • Indian Masala ingredients
  • Mustard Oil
  • Curd

Steps to be followed:

  1. Wash vegetables with running water. Make sure veggies are clean.
  2. Cut all vegetables
  3. Take oil in cooker and heat
  4. Put garlic and Onion in oil and fry
  5. Fry for 1-2 minutes and make sure it not deep fried
  6. Mix masala with fried ingredients and coook for 4-5 minutes till masala starts smelling good
  7. Cook for 5 mintues on slow flame
  8. Now mix veggies in masala and keep stirring on mid falme for 3-4 minutes
  9. Now pour sufficient water and keep in mind green veggies also have water so don't add excess water in rice
  10. Wait till cooker makes pressure and whistles

Aha! Ready to eat
